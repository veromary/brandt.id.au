EDU2.0 is a Learning Management System. We are trialling their free account with room for up to 2000 students (so if you want to join in the experiement, just ask!)

Currently our courses include: Catholic's Latin Instructor chapters (Benediction and Altar Server Latin), ML Nesbit's Grammar Land and CP Bowler's Living the Truth. We take an old text book and put together online quizzes so students can log in and complete assessments to show their mastery of the content.

There is a special account for parents, but as home educators you may prefer to join as a teacher instead. That way you will have more access to the course materials and even be able to develop your own courses.
